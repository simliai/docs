### Supported Emotions
The Simli app currently supports 5 distinct emotions that can be applied to your avatar, but our APIs support even more. 

## Available Emotions

Simli provides a comprehensive set of emotions to make your avatar more expressive and engaging. Each emotion has multiple variations to provide natural diversity in expressions.

### Preset Emotions

| Emotion | Description | 
|---------|-------------|
| **Natural** | Default, calm expressions | 
| **Happy** | Positive, joyful expressions | 
| **Angry** | Frustrated, upset expressions | 
| **Doubtful** | Uncertain, skeptical expressions |
